About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Wisconsin Center for Education Research, University of Wisconsin-Madison. My work explores equity-centered leadership preparation, Islamic education, digital pedagogy, and computational research methods. My research employs mixed methods, including social network analysis, topic modeling, and qualitative coding.

Research Interests

  • Equity-centered school leadership
  • Islamic education in Southeast Asia
  • Social network analysis in education
  • Topic modeling and AI in education
  • Religious moderation and public policy
